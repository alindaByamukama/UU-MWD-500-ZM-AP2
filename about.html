Our Mission & History

Since 1985, our department has been dedicated to excellence in teaching and research in computer science. We are proud of our legacy and excited about the future of computer science at our university.

Our Mission

Our mission is to advance the field of computer science through innovative research and to provide high-quality education that prepares students for successful careers in technology. We strive to foster a diverse and inclusive environment that encourages collaboration and creativity. We are committed to providing our students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in a rapidly changing technological landscape.

Our History

Established in 1985, our department has grown from a small group of faculty and students to a vibrant community of scholars and researchers. We have consistently ranked among the top computer science programs in the country, with a strong emphasis on research and innovation. Our faculty members are leaders in their fields, and our students are engaged in cutting-edge research across a wide range of topics.
